Cumulative NIH Biomarker Research Spending, FY2004–2024

NIH has spent between $36B and $134B on biomarker-related research since 2004, depending on how broadly "biomarker" is defined. The gap reflects spending on omics and imaging studies with clinical intent that may not use explicit biomarker terminology.
