# NIH Biomarker Research Funding Analysis

Analysis of NIH Reporter data (October 2024 export) examining funding patterns for biomarker-related research from FY1988-2024.

## Dataset Overview

**Source**: NIH Reporter exports filtered by biomarker-related keywords
- **Total Projects**: 24,837 unique project-year combinations
- **Time Span**: FY1988 - FY2024 (36 years)
- **Data Size**: 60.6 MB
- **Search Terms Used**:
  - "biomarker validation"
  - "surrogate marker", "intermediate endpoint", "endophenotype", "surrogate biomarker"

**Top 5 NIH Institutes (overall dataset)**:
1. NCI - 6,999 projects
2. NIA - 2,809 projects
